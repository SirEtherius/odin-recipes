Delicious lasagna


Delicous lasagna dish

Description

This recipe will walk you through an ancient recipe from ancient Rome, Italy. It uses methods used by the Romans in the late 16th Century.

Brace yourself as we embark on this culinary adventure. Your taste buds will travel back in time as you savour this magnificent lasagna

Ingredients
  • 5kg Italian Tomatoes
  • 15 dried lasagna sheets
  • Parsley
  • minced pork meat
  • Italian Provolone cheese
  • Milk
Directions
  1. Mash all the tomatoes in a large bucket using your bare feet
  2. Make a fire (we recommend using sticks and grindstone)
  3. Cook the meat on a large stone tablet
  4. Form lasagna layers with cooked meat, tomato, pasta and cheese
  5. Put lasagna in oven until cheese browns on top
